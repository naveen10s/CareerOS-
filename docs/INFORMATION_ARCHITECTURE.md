# Information Architecture Spec - CareerOS

This document specifies the global navigation hierarchy and structural inventory for **CareerOS**, detailing how information is organized to serve the target audience.

---

## 🗺️ 1. Navigation & Content Hierarchy

```text
Root ("/")
├── Home / Landing Page
├── Career Dashboard ("/dashboard")
├── Journey Timeline ("/journey")
├── Experience Matrix ("/experience")
├── Projects Case Studies ("/projects")
├── Product Lab Workspace ("/product-lab")
├── Analytics Telemetry ("/analytics")
├── Learning Engine ("/learning")
├── Resumé Portal ("/resume")
└── Contact Desk ("/contact")
```

---

## 📋 2. Page Inventory

### 1. Home / Landing Page (`/`)

- **Purpose**: Establish value proposition immediately; provide instant access to the Command Bar shortcut, and route to specific candidate evaluation points.
- **Target Users**: Recruiters, Hiring Managers, Founders, Product Leaders.
- **Business Goal**: Maximize visitor retention, generate curiosity, and drive click rates into the Career Dashboard.
- **Primary CTA**: Launch Dashboard (`/dashboard`) or Open Command Bar (`Ctrl+K`).

### 2. Career Dashboard (`/dashboard`)

- **Purpose**: Serve as a high-fidelity cockpit displaying summary metrics (skills progress, projects completed, transition velocity) to mirror actual SaaS platforms.
- **Target Users**: Hiring Managers, Founders, Product Leaders.
- **Business Goal**: Demonstrate operational product metrics management, backlog agility, and data literacy.
- **Primary CTA**: Explore Journey Timeline (`/journey`).

### 3. Journey Timeline (`/journey`)

- **Purpose**: Map the sequential progression from Business Analyst roles to Product Management milestones. Highlight overlap achievements (e.g. telemetry improvements).
- **Target Users**: Recruiter, Hiring Manager.
- **Business Goal**: Tell a structured career story, establishing BA competencies (SQL, process mapping) as transferable PM skills.
- **Primary CTA**: View Project Case Studies (`/projects`).

### 4. Experience Matrix (`/experience`)

- **Purpose**: Standardize professional career timeline, with filter toggles showing "BA Responsibilities" vs "PM Initatives".
- **Target Users**: Hiring Managers, recruiters.
- **Business Goal**: Frame career tenure as a series of customer-centric optimizations rather than legacy analyst task descriptions.
- **Primary CTA**: View Resumé (`/resume`).

### 5. Projects Case Studies (`/projects`)

- **Purpose**: House deep-dive case studies covering product launches, wireframes, agile roadmaps, and business solutions.
- **Target Users**: Product Leaders, Engineering Managers.
- **Business Goal**: Show engineering collaboration skills, wireframing capabilities, and product requirement document (PRD) drafting.
- **Primary CTA**: Open Product Lab Workspace (`/product-lab`).

### 6. Product Lab Workspace (`/product-lab`)

- **Purpose**: Active playground displaying interactive product design mockups, roadmap gantt charts, and sprint backlog tickets.
- **Target Users**: Engineering Managers, Product Leaders.
- **Business Goal**: Demonstrate absolute comfort with design handoffs, ticket sizing, and backlog grooming.
- **Primary CTA**: Explore Performance Analytics (`/analytics`).

### 7. Analytics Telemetry (`/analytics`)

- **Purpose**: Display live metric telemetry reports showing cohort charts, performance latencies, and conversion funnels from past initiatives.
- **Target Users**: Product Leaders, Founders, CTO.
- **Business Goal**: Prove deep telemetry knowledge and data-driven product prioritization skills.
- **Primary CTA**: View Learning Path (`/learning`).

### 8. Learning Engine (`/learning`)

- **Purpose**: Document current educational efforts, courses, certifications, and product readings.
- **Target Users**: Hiring Managers, recruiters.
- **Business Goal**: Display growth mindset and active upskilling during transition phases.
- **Primary CTA**: Check Certifications (`/learning`).

### 9. Resumé Portal (`/resume`)

- **Purpose**: Display structured, printable one-page resumé optimizing both BA tenure and PM transition goals.
- **Target Users**: Recruiters.
- **Business Goal**: Facilitate candidate shortlisting and exportable PDF downloads.
- **Primary CTA**: Download PDF Resumé.

### 10. Contact Desk (`/contact`)

- **Purpose**: Establish direct communication links with scheduling calendars and calendar availability widgets.
- **Target Users**: Recruiters, Hiring Managers, Founders.
- **Business Goal**: Secure interviews and direct outreach.
- **Primary CTA**: Book Interview Slot.
